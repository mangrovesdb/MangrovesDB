Blockchain Netwroks on MGDB
==============================

In this section we cover the important blockchain networks which are supported in the MangrovesDB are discussed. mangroves has the following essential features which makes it attaptable with almost all public and private blockchain network.

- A **Postgres-compatible interface** that allows seamless integration with various programming languages and ``third-party dashboard or analytical services`` such as Grafana, making it accessible to a diverse range of users.
- Easy-to-use SQL commands for ``dApp developers``, simplifying the process of writing applications and interacting with blockchain networks.
- MGDB is **Self-deployable** and can connect to clients' ``private blockchain`` nodes.
- **Self-deployable** functionality, enabling MangrovesDB to connect to clients' private blockchain nodes and cater to their unique requirements.
- Acting as a **gateway to multiple public and private blockchain networks**, MangrovesDB supports a variety of platforms such as Ethereum, Polygon, and Hyperledger, offering flexibility and adaptability for diverse use cases.
- The ability to **develop smart contracts leveraging relational database features**, allowing users to take advantage of the power and flexibility of SQL while working with blockchain networks.

The initial version of the mangroves can interact with Ethereum-compatible peojects, Solana and Hyperledger Fabric networks.
